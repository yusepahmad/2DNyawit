# Sawit2D: Official Gameplay Guide

Welcome to **Sawit2D** (2D Nyawit), a top-down simulation game where you manage a palm oil plantation. Your goal is to build a successful farm, manage risks, and grow your economy while navigating environmental challenges.

---

## 1. Getting Started: The First Day

When you start a new game, you begin at your **Home** (located at the top right of the map).

- **Default Equipment**: You start with one **Axe** in your inventory.
- **The Garden**: The plantation area is initially overgrown with **Dry Trees** arranged in an organic forest pattern — not every tile has a tree, leaving natural clearings.
- **Initial Task**: Clear the land before you can plant anything.

### Equipping the Axe

1. Press **C** to open the Inventory.
2. Select the **Axe** using W/A/S/D then press **Enter** to equip it.
3. Your character's sprite will change to show them carrying the axe while walking.
4. Select the Axe again to **unequip** it and return to the normal walking sprite.

### How to Clear Land

1. Approach a **Dry Tree** in the garden area.
2. Press **E** or **Enter** to chop it down.
3. The tree will turn into a **Trunk**.
4. **Wait 1 Day**: Trunks decay after one in-game day. Once the day changes, the trunk disappears, leaving **Plantable Soil**.

---

## 2. Farming Mechanics

### Planting

- **Requirements**: Have **Bibit Sawit** (Palm Seeds) in your inventory.
- **Action**: Stand on a cleared soil tile and press **E** or **Enter** to plant.
- **Cost**: Each planting consumes 1 seed.

### Growth

Palm oil plants grow through 3 stages:

1. **Stage 1** — Small sprout.
2. **Stage 2** — Developing plant.
3. **Stage 3** — Ready for harvest (large palm fruit visible).

> Growth happens during the daily transition (press **N** to advance the day).

### Harvesting

Stand near a Stage 3 palm plant and press **E** or **Enter**. You will receive **1 TBS** (Tandan Buah Segar / Fresh Fruit Bunch) added to your inventory.

---

## 3. Economy & The Market

### The Market (Pasar)

The Market is located at the **top left of the map**, connected to your home by a road.

- **Access**: Press **M** while near the Market building to open the menu.
- **Buying Seeds**: Buy **Bibit Sawit** for **$30** each. Select quantity with W/S then press **Enter**.
- **Buying Loudspeaker**: Buy the **Loudspeaker** for **$200** (see Section 5).
- **Selling Harvest**: Sell your collected **TBS** for **$120** each. Press **F** to sell all at once.
- **Exit**: Press **ESC** or **M** to close the market.

### Managing Gold

- Your current Gold is displayed at the **top right** of the HUD.
- **Income**: Selling TBS is your primary source of income.
- **Expenses**: Seeds, Loudspeaker, firebreaks, and assistance calls all cost gold.

### Overview HUD

A small **Overview panel** is always visible at the top left during gameplay showing:
- Total TBS harvested and sold this session
- Number of active palm plots
- Number of plots ready to harvest

## 4. Risks & Disasters

### Fire Risk

The risk of fire increases based on the **size of your connected plantation clusters**.

- **Risk Score**: View your current risk in the **Farm Status** panel (open with **C**).
- **Spontaneous Combustion**: High risk can trigger fires during the night.

### Fire Spread

- Uncontrolled fires spread to adjacent tiles every day.
- Each spread costs **$40** in losses.

### Firebreaks

- **Action**: Press **B** while standing on a tile to toggle a Firebreak.
- **Cost**: **$20** per firebreak.
- **Function**: Stops fire spread and significantly reduces your plantation's risk score.

### Firefighters

When a fire disaster occurs, an event window will appear:

1. **Handle Manually** — Costs gold to call for immediate assistance.
2. **Wait for Rain** — Risky; fire may spread before rain arrives.
3. **Government Assistance** — Occasionally, public figures (Verrell Bramasta or Zulhas) may arrive to help.

---

## 5. The Loudspeaker

The **Loudspeaker** is a special equippable item available at the Market for **$200**.

### Equipping

1. Buy it at the Market.
2. Open Inventory (**C**), select the Loudspeaker, press **Enter** to equip.
3. Your character's walking sprite will change to a special Loudspeaker pose.
4. Select it again to unequip.

### Using the Loudspeaker

| Key | Effect |
| :--- | :--- |
| **F** | Plays *"Hey Antek-Antek Asing"* audio |
| **G** | Plays *"Hidup Jokowi"* audio with an on-screen notification |

---

## 6. Weather: Rain

Rain is a dynamic system that impacts gameplay:

- **Pros**: Automatically extinguishes all fires and resets fire risk to zero.
- **Cons**: Reduces TBS sell price by **30%** (multiplier 0.7) due to transport difficulties.

---

## 7. Automation & Services

As you progress, you can toggle automated services:

| Key | Service |
| :--- | :--- |
| **H** | Toggle **Auto Plant** — automatically plants seeds in available plots |
| **K** | Toggle **Auto Harvest** — automatically harvests ready plants |
| **J** | Toggle **Auto Sell** — automatically sells TBS when the transport truck arrives |

---

## 8. Achievements

The game includes a hidden achievement system. Progress is **saved automatically** between sessions and **resets** when you start a New Game.

- Open the **Pause Menu** (**P** or **ESC**) → select **Achievements** to view your progress.
- The screen shows how many achievements you have unlocked out of the total.
- Unlocked achievements show their **name and description**.
- Locked achievements show only a **hint/clue** to help you discover them by playing.
- A notification banner appears on screen whenever you unlock an achievement.

> There are **9 achievements** to discover. Explore every feature of the game to find them all.

## 10. Game Over Conditions

- **Bankruptcy**: Gold reaches **$0** or below.
- **Land Seizure**: No planting for **14 consecutive days** — the government reclaims your land.

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Clear land in batches**: Trunks take a day to decay, so chop trees the day before you plan to plant.
2. **Don't build too big too fast**: Large connected clusters increase fire risk. Use firebreaks to break up sections.
3. **Watch the weather**: Sell your harvest on sunny days for full price.
4. **Keep seeds in stock**: Always have a reserve so you don't trigger the 14-day vacancy game over.
5. **Explore the map**: The forest area has natural clearings — some spots are ready to plant immediately.
6. **Check Farm Status**: Open **C** during gameplay to see detailed risk, reputation, and automation status.

Good luck, and happy farming!
