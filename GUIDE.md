# Sawit2D: Official Gameplay Guide

Welcome to **Sawit2D** (2D Nyawit), a top-down simulation game where you manage a palm oil plantation. Your goal is to build a successful farm, manage risks, and grow your economy while navigating environmental challenges.

---

## 1. Getting Started: The First Day
When you start a new game, you begin at your **Home** (located at the top right of the map).
*   **Default Equipment**: You start with one **Axe**.
*   **The Garden**: The plantation area is initially overgrown with **Dry Trees**.
*   **Initial Task**: You must clear the land before you can plant anything.

### How to Clear Land:
1.  Approach a **Dry Tree** in the garden area.
2.  Press **E** or **Enter** to chop it down.
3.  The tree will turn into a **Trunk**.
4.  **Wait for 1 Day**: Trunks take one in-game day to decay. Once the day changes (by sleeping or waiting), the trunk will disappear, leaving behind **Plantable Soil**.

---

## 2. Farming Mechanics

### Planting
*   **Requirements**: You must have **Bibit Sawit** (Palm Seeds) in your inventory.
*   **Action**: Stand on a cleared soil tile and press **E** or **Enter** to plant.
*   **Cost**: Each planting action consumes 1 seed.

### Growth
Palm oil plants grow through 3 stages:
1.  **Stage 1**: Small sprout.
2.  **Stage 2**: Developing plant.
3.  **Stage 3**: Ready for harvest (large palm fruit visible).
*   *Note: Growth happens during the daily transition.*

### Harvesting
*   Stand near a Stage 3 palm plant and press **E** or **Enter**.
*   You will receive **1 TBS** (Tandan Buah Segar / Fresh Fruit Bunch) in your inventory.

---

## 3. Economy & The Market

### The Market (Pasar)
The Market is located at the top left of the map, connected to your home by a road.
*   **Access**: Press **M** while near the Market building to open the menu.
*   **Buying Seeds**: Buy **Bibit Sawit** for **$30** each.
*   **Selling Harvest**: Sell your collected **TBS** for **$120** each.

### Managing Gold
*   Your current Gold is displayed at the top right of the HUD.
*   **Income**: Selling TBS is your primary source of income.
*   **Expenses**: Buying seeds and calling for assistance (like firefighters or gardeners) costs gold.

---

## 4. Risks & Disasters

### Fire Risk
The risk of fire increases based on the **size of your connected plantation clusters**.
*   **Risk Score**: View your current risk in the Farm Status box (top left).
*   **Spontaneous Combustion**: High risk levels can trigger fires during the night.

### Fire Spread
*   **Spread**: Uncontrolled fires spread to adjacent tiles every day.
*   **Losses**: Each tile that catches fire results in lost crops and a financial penalty (**$40 per spread**).

### Firebreaks
*   **Action**: Press **B** while standing on a tile to install/remove a **Firebreak**.
*   **Cost**: **$20** per firebreak.
*   **Function**: Firebreaks stop the spread of fire and significantly reduce the overall risk score of your plantation.

### Firefighters
If a disaster occurs, an event window will appear. You can choose to:
1.  **Handle Manually**: Costs gold to call for immediate assistance.
2.  **Wait for Rain**: Risky, as fire may spread before rain arrives.
3.  **Government Assistance**: Occasionally, public figures (Verrell Bramasta or Zulhas) may arrive to help or provide relief.

---

## 5. Weather: Rain
Rain is a dynamic system that impacts gameplay:
*   **Pros**: Rain automatically extinguishes all fires and reduces fire risk to zero.
*   **Cons**: Rain makes transportation difficult, reducing the sell price of TBS by **30%** (multiplier 0.7).

---

## 6. Automation & Services

### Gardener Services (Auto Modes)
As you progress, you can toggle automated services:
*   **Auto Plant (H)**: Automatically plants seeds in available plots.
*   **Auto Harvest (K)**: Automatically harvests ready plants.
*   **Auto Sell (J)**: Automatically sells TBS from your inventory when the transport truck arrives.

---

## 7. Controls Summary

| Key | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **W, A, S, D** | Move Character |
| **E / Enter** | Interact / Plant / Harvest / Chop Tree |
| **M** | Open Market (must be near Market) |
| **C** | Open Character / Inventory |
| **B** | Toggle Firebreak |
| **N** | Advance to Next Day |
| **Q** | Quick Sell Stock |
| **P / ESC** | Pause Game |
| **F** | Attack (requires weapon) |

---

## 8. Game Over Conditions
*   **Bankruptcy**: If your Gold reaches **$0** or below, the game ends.
*   **Land Seizure**: If you do not plant anything for **14 consecutive days**, the government will seize your land for being unproductive.

---

## Tips for Success
1.  **Don't build too big too fast**: Large clusters of plants are fire magnets. Use firebreaks to split your plantation into smaller sections.
2.  **Clear land in advance**: Don't wait until you run out of space to chop trees, as they take a day to clear.
3.  **Watch the weather**: Try to sell your harvest on sunny days for maximum profit.
4.  **Keep seeds in stock**: Always have a reserve of seeds so you don't trigger the "14-day vacancy" game over.

Good luck, and happy farming!
